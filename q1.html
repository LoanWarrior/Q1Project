why?

Ever been looking for parking for an hour and just decide to drive off a cliff instead? No more. We developed what we call "monkeys" technology that allows us to map the entire world of parking!

how?

How you ask? Well, it's a funny story. Once upon a time at band camp, a group of monkeys were found in the wilderness. Nobody speaks monkey, but the band kids figured out somehow that they love to color maps. Who knew! With this newfound technology in hand, we've accomplished so much in so little time. With a team of 16 monkeys on the job, they were able to map out all the parking on this site. Without them, our algorithm would be toast.

what?

What is right! Right indeed. Imagine you desparately are looking for parking, and a monkey jumps out into the road. Why did this happen? How do you react? The best thing to do is grab it and cage it up for proper use, but that's our job. Your job is to find parking. Note: no animals have been harmed by Edison Co. or any of its' affiliates.
